What's Covered?

Breathe easy knowing your beloved two-wheeler is protected against a wide range of eventualities:

  • Accidents:Collisions, mishaps, unfortunate bumps? No worries. Get coverage for repair costs, ensuring your bike gets back to its prime quickly.
  • Theft:Losing your bike is a devastating experience. We offer compensation for your stolen vehicle, helping you recover financially and get back on two wheels sooner.
  • Fire:Flames are no match for your peace of mind. We cover fire damage to your bike, minimizing the financial burden of such unfortunate incidents.
  • Natural Disasters:Floods, cyclones, and other forces of nature can leave a trail of damage. We provide coverage against losses caused by natural disasters, keeping you protected even in the face of the unexpected.
  • Personal Accident:Accidents can happen even to the most cautious riders. We offer coverage for medical expenses and injuries sustained in an accident, ensuring you receive the care you need.
  • Third-Party Liabilities:Accidental damage to another person's vehicle, property, or even causing physical harm can lead to hefty financial burdens. Our third-party liability coverage shields you from such situations, providing peace of mind and legal compliance.

Yes, in India, at least a third-party insurance policy is required by law. Riding without insurance can lead to hefty fines and legal repercussions.

âž¢ What documents do I need to buy insurance?

You'll typically need your driver's license, registration certificate (RC) of your bike, previous year's insurance policy (if applicable), and address proof (ID card, Aadhaar card, etc.).

âž¢ Can I customize my insurance plan?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of add-ons, allowing you to personalize your coverage based on your needs and budget. Whether you want zero depreciation cover for full repair value, return to invoice protection for complete loss scenarios, or breakdown assistance for roadside emergencies, we have you covered.

Filing a claim with PremiumBima is quick and easy. Simply go online or use our mobile app to submit your claim, upload pictures of the damage, and track the progress in real-time. We believe in a transparent and efficient claims process, getting you back on the road faster.

âž¢ How long does it take to settle a claim?

âž¢ What happens if I don't file a claim for several years?

Maintaining a clean claim history is rewarded! By opting for rides and avoiding mishaps, you'll be eligible for a No Claim Bonus (NCB). This translates to attractive discounts on your insurance renewal premiums, year after year. So, ride responsibly and reap the benefits of careful riding.

âž¢ What are the different types of two-wheeler insurance policies?

The two main types of two-wheeler insurance are:

  • Third-Party: This mandatory policy covers damages caused to third parties, including their vehicles, property, and even injuries. It's a basic and affordable option that fulfills legal requirements.
  • Comprehensive: This plan goes beyond the law, protecting both your own bike (in case of accidents, theft, fire, etc.) and third-party liabilities. It provides complete peace of mind and is particularly valuable for expensive bikes or riders seeking maximum protection.

âž¢ Can I choose my own repair garage ?

Yes, you have the flexibility to choose any authorized repair garage. However, opting for our cashless network garages provides added convenience. You won't need to pay upfront repair costs, streamlining the process and saving you time and hassle.

âž¢ What happens if my bike is stolen?

If your bike is stolen and covered under your policy (comprehensive plans typically include theft coverage), you'll receive compensation based on the Insured Declared Value (IDV) mentioned in your policy. This value represents the bike's market value at the time of insurance purchase.

âž¢ Do I need to renew my insurance every year?

Yes, two-wheeler insurance policies are valid for one year only and need to be renewed before they expire to maintain coverage. Don't let your protection lapse - remember to renew your policy promptly to avoid any risks.
